# Power-BI
🛒 Blinkit Sales & Performance Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze Blinkit's grocery sales performance, customer preferences, outlet efficiency, and product trends. The dashboard transforms raw business data into actionable insights that support strategic decision-making and performance monitoring.

The objective of this project is to identify key sales drivers, evaluate outlet performance, understand customer purchasing behavior, and provide data-driven recommendations for business growth.

# 🎯 Business Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance across different outlets.
Identify top-performing cities and outlet types.
Understand product category contribution to total sales.
Compare sales across outlet locations and sizes.
Track key business KPIs for informed decision-making.
Discover trends and opportunities for business expansion.

📈 Key Business Insights
🔹 Top Performing Markets

Bangalore, Chennai, and Mumbai emerged as the highest revenue-generating cities, highlighting strong customer demand and market potential.

🔹 Tier-3 Market Opportunity

Tier-3 locations generated higher sales compared to Tier-1 cities, suggesting significant growth opportunities in less competitive regions.

🔹 Health-Conscious Consumer Trend

Low-fat products contribute approximately 64.6% of total sales, indicating increasing consumer preference for healthier food options.

🔹 Revenue Driving Categories

Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are the major contributors to overall sales and should remain a priority for inventory planning.

🔹 Best Performing Outlet Type

Supermarket Type 1 consistently outperformed other outlet formats, making it a strong candidate for future expansion strategies.
